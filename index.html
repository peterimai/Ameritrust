Ameritrust Tulsa is a certified NAPFA Fee Only Financial Planning firm. Serving Tulsa since 1991.

Investment Management

Fee-only financial planners are registered investment advisors with a fiduciary responsibility to act in their clients' best interest. They do not accept any fees or compensation based on product sales. Fee-only advisors have fewer inherent conflicts of interest, and they generally provide more comprehensive advice.

A Message from our President

In 1997, I established The AmeriTrust Companies because I saw a need for a different kind of company that provides client-focused financial services with an emphasis on personal attention and superior customer service. We provide professional trust administration, financial planning and investment management services to our clients with a personal touch.

Our approach is different because all of our services are offered on a fee-only basis. This simply means that we provide objective, honest and unbiased advice to our clients since we never work on commission or sell any type of financial products. In 1982, I became the first fee-only financial planner registered in the state of Oklahoma with NAPFA (link to napfa.org), the national organization for fee-only planners. Our commitment to providing fee-only services sets us apart and helps our clients trust our advice.

Our team is different because our associates are passionate about helping our clients. Many of our associates have been working together since 1987 when we were part of the Fourth National Bank of Tulsa's Trust Department (now Bank of America). I also encourage my employees to give back to the Tulsa community and as an example I serve on many nonprofit boards and support many local causes.

Our results are different because we take the long-term view and emphasize a "value investment" approach to preserving and growing your wealth. We link our success to your success. We deliver a highly personalized service, tailored specifically for you that focuses strictly on your financial goals.

These long-term relationships are at the heart of why AmeriTrust exists and we understand how precious this gift of trust is and take our role very seriously to do our best to be there for each client today, tomorrow and into the future.
